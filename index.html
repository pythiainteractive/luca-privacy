LUCA Privacy Policy

Information Collection

LUCA collects anonymized data to improve functionality and user experience. This includes usage statistics, crash reports, and basic device information (e.g., device model, operating system, and app version). No personally identifiable information is collected.

Data Storage

All calculations, buffers, and preferences you enter are securely stored locally on your device. However, anonymized usage data collected for analytics and crash reporting is processed by Firebase, a third-party service provided by Google. This data is used solely for app improvement and is not shared with other parties.

Third-Party Services

LUCA uses Firebase for analytics and crash reporting. Firebase collects and processes anonymized data, such as app usage and crash details, in compliance with their privacy policy. For more details, please review Firebase's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

To improve LUCA's functionality and user experience, we collect anonymized usage data through basic analytics. This data does not include any personally identifiable information and is used solely for app improvement purposes.

In-App Purchases

LUCA offers in-app purchases that function as voluntary donations to support the app's development. These purchases are processed through Apple's App Store and are subject to Apple's privacy policy and terms of service.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the app or applicable laws. Any updates will be communicated within the app. Continued use of LUCA following an update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: luca@pythiainteractive.com
